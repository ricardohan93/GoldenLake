History

The birth of the club

In 2005, the former Lake Said Golfe Clube was incoporated by Golden Lake, passing through administrative changes and structural renovations.


In 2012, the new headquarter was ready. With a wide space, modern locker rooms, sauna, restaurant and parking lot, the Golden Lake Golf Club assure your promisse of continuous improvement and quality
